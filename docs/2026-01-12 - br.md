# Pulse Patrol

>Business requirements

- Collects data about patients: medical records, test results, admission forms, etc.
- Collects data from medical equipment used for investigations.
- The data is accessible to patients through a web application.
- Doctors and authorized personnel have access to the data of patients admitted to the hospitals where they work.
- The system allows for alerting medical staff when monitoring systems detect abnormal values.
- The system can be sold to various healthcare companies and facilitates the transfer of patients from one to another.
